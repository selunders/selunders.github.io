'Them's Fighting Words'

CS383 - Software Engineering

Fall 2021

Them's Fighting Words | Fall 2021

View Repo

This project was my first experience working with both C# and Unity. I worked on a team of six on this semester-long project in which each team member had a feature they 'championed', as well as a 2-3 week window as the team lead.


The idea behind the game was to make a 2D overworld where you talk to NPCs, then—on purpose or not—you say something that upsets them, and they challenge you to various arcade-style games (think Snake, Brick-Breaker, Street-Fighter, etc).


My feature was the controls. This was fairly straightforward once I began to learn about the Unity engine.


I was the final team lead, which means I was in charge of getting everyone's features working well together. We exported the game for Windows, Linux, and Android.


We didn't get nearly as much completed as we hoped, but if you want to check out our end result, here's our downloads page.
